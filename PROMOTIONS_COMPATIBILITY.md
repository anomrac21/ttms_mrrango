## ⚠️ Limitations

1. **Time-based promos** – Happy Hour (5–8 PM) and Tequila Shots (5–8 PM) show all day. Staff should apply correct times at POS.
2. **Fixed amounts** – $20 OFF and combo prices are approximated with percent discount where possible. Exact amounts may need manual handling.
3. **Wings "5 free with 10"** – Different from standard BOGO. Label displays correctly; discount logic may need staff verification.
